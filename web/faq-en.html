Planning poker FAQ

Quick answers about rooms, invites, estimates, and exports.

Try it free

  • What is SpritzPlanning?

    An online planning poker app for software teams. Open a room, add stories to estimate, and vote together in real time. The UI uses bar metaphors (room, barman, drinks) — no mandatory login.

  • Do I need an account?

    No. Enter a nickname (at least 2 characters) and the room code. You can start a scrum poker session in under a minute.

  • How do I invite my team?

    Share the room code, use the QR code in the room, or send the short link /j/CODE. On mobile, the link opens the join form directly.

  • Who is the barman?

    The barman facilitates the session: manages the backlog, starts voting, reveals votes, and confirms the final estimate. The role can be transferred to another participant.

  • Which estimation decks are available?

    Default is Fibonacci (0, ½, 1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 13, 21, ?, ☕). The barman can pick presets or custom values for the room.

  • Can I export estimates after a session?

    Yes. The session summary exports as CSV (spreadsheets) or Markdown (wikis, notes) — handy after sprint planning.

  • Does it work on phone and tablet?

    Yes. SpritzPlanning is a responsive web app. Install it as a PWA from your browser for quick access.

  • Is SpritzPlanning really free?

    Yes for standard use: create rooms, invite your team, and estimate at no cost. The goal is a lightweight story pointing tool, not a heavy enterprise platform.

  • Can I join as an observer only?

    Yes. On join, expand Advanced options and enable observe-only mode: you watch without voting (useful for stakeholders or new team members).
